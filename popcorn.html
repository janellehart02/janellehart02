How To Make Popcorn From Scratch

Directions

For the most buttery and delicious popcorn, follow the steps below:

  1. Add 1/4 cup of vegetable oil (or any high heat oil) to a deep pan, or enough to thinly layer the bottom of the pan.
  2. Add 3/4 cup of popcorn kernels, cover the pan with a lid, and turn the stove on high heat.
  3. When you hear popping begin, shake the pan side to side to prevent the kernels from sticking to the bottom and burning.
  4. Continue to shake every 20 to 30 seconds until you hear the popping slow down.
  5. Turn off the heat and pour the popcorn into a large, deep bowl.
  6. Melt 4 tbsps of salted butter in the microwave for 30 seconds and then pour over the popcorn in increments, shaking the bowl in between rounds.
  7. Add a few pinches of salt (or whatever spices you prefer), mix, and serve.

*A few other toppings I recommend are melted dark chocolate with flaky sea salt, olive oil with rosemary and garlic seasoning, chili lime seasoning, or chipotle seasoning.

Health Benefits Of Eating Popcorn

    Since it is whole grain, popcorn is linked to better blood circulation and digestive health.

    Studies have found that popcorn can potentially lower risks of certain cancers.

    Popcorn is high in fiber, thus preventing high cholesterol and heart disease.
